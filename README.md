# Backyard Dirt Removal


------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Open Backyard

-   58 ft x 15 ft\ (870 sq ft)
-   Open backyard (no patio, turf, or sheds)
-   No known gas, water, or electrical lines
-   volume of dirt: 11--22 cubic yard


 ### Access: 3-foot wide fence gate


## Scope of Work

-   Remove **4 to 8 inches** of soil (depth varies due to slope)
-   Remove surface layer of **pebbles and small blocks**
-   Perform **light leveling/grading** to reduce slope
-   Protect one existing **aciment tree**
-   Haul away and dispose of all removed dirt and debris
-   Must avoid damage to underground **drain duct**
